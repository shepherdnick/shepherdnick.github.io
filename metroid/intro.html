YOU ARE IN: METROID PRIME: INTRODUCTION

I hear you asking "WHAT IN THE WORLD IS METROID PRIME?".  Well my friend, you have started in the right place.  This web site is designed to explain to you what the game is about!  We'll have to start with, I'll describe the background knowledge needed to understand the rest of the game.

Who are the Chozo?

Through the years, the Chozo have used their intelligence to make incredible technological and scientific advances and making many useful changes throughout the universe.  In every planet they visited, they built cities and lived in harmony with more primitive creatures with which they shared their intelligence and technology.  This bird-like population learned to respect and nurture life in all forms.

Intelligent though they were, the Chozo race reached a development peak.  Their culture went down in history as being known as spiritual and peaceful, but using their wisdom, they looked into the future and saw the decline of their race.  The increase in evil and violence in the universe scared them - forcing them to go into hiding and substituting using their technology for minimalism. They hid on the planet of Tallon IV (just one of the places they found refuge) and built a settlement which benefited the planet, creating a peaceful environment for both creatures and the land.

Time went on, and the Chozo lived happily on Tallon IV until one day a meteor crashed onto the surface of the planet.  This sent a huge mass of dust into the atmosphere and released a cancerous poison called Phazon.  Phazon infected all creatures and plants that inhabitted the planet, and either killed them or mutated them.

Tried though they would, the Chozo failed to maintain or control the spread of Phazon, and in a last attempt before they left, they built a temple over the crater that the meteor made, in hope that it would seal the heart of the disease.  They left the planet and prayed that a savior would come, only leaving their knowledge and technological attempts on the planet.

What happened next?

Space Pirates - evil interstellar creatures who were powerful with weaponry and advanced with space travel technology, took control of the Metroids (discovered by the Galactic Federation) as they looked good for military resources.  They wiped out planets, like Zebes, killing most of the Chozo which lived there and built research laboratories underground.  They used these research laboratories for some years, testing on the Metroids and learning more about them.

After attacks on K-2L, orphaned, and lonely, a little girl was taken in by the Chozo.  Her name - Samus Aran.  She lived and trained as a warrior, and donned a Chozo made suit to project her.    Once ready, she went on a valiant quest to destroy the Space Pirate empire on Zebes.  After successfully infiltrating the base, she destroyed all the Metroids and the "Mother Brain" - the leader of the Metroids.

The Space Pirates were not stopped though, and split into two groups - one destined to rebuild the bases on Zebes, and the other to search for a planet rich with resources.  Tallon IV was the place they reached, as it wasn't hard to spot.  High levels of energy were being emitted from the planet, because of the Phazon.  It was here they were going to build a massive underground network of laboratories and life support systems.

Their research revealed that Phazon had a capacity to mutate life forms to an extent unseen by them before.  They perfected and controlled the procedures and were able to mine the Phazon.  They transported it to Phendrana Drifts, where the specimens were easier to contain.  They enhanced the power of Phazon and created grotesque beasts which were released all over the planet of Tallon IV.

They shipped some of their Phazon to a space station orbiting the planet for Zero Gravity experiments.  Little did they know, the infamous Samus Aran was on their tails, and is where we begin our story...
