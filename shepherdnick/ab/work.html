What my life seems to mainly consist of. See I work for the multinational corporation that provides food at a fast speed to a large group of customers.

Yes! That's right. I work for McDonalds.

And to be honest, it's not bad working there at all. I enjoy working with the people there (with the exception of a few who don't know me). And the pay isn't bad. I can't complain about that. I usually work about 3 shifts a week, that's one in the weekday evening, and two on the weekend.

All I can say about disregarding any rumours you may think about McDonalds is that it is the most hygenic restaurants I have ever worked in. And I've worked in a few. There are no 10 second rules there... and the mayo is in a sealed container.

During the summer holidays instead of sitting on my arse all day, I usually work with my Dad. Well it's not technically with him, because I work in the labs in the same factory. For me its a great chance to learn more about science and how to use scientific equipment.

My job usually involves testing product before it's allowed to be sold to the shops. I usually also take part in some product development, which means I have to simulate making things like body lotions, shower gels, shampoos, colognes and other toiletries etc.
