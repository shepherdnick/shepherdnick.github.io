Tha Cewl Crew

Whilst I was in West Mon I made friends with 6 other people. We were the most mixed bunch of people in the school, but we all enjoyed each others company. Called by many others in the school as being the Cool Crew, the name sort of stuck and we always got known by it.

Who?

Well apart from me there was Ashley, Tom, Jon, Rachel, Dave and Robyn.

Ashley

When I think of Ashley, I think Bass Guitar and Double Bass, and so should you. He doesn't stop playing either instrument and as far as we can see, he doesn't love much else (apart from El).

I can guarantee that Ashley will always be up for a laugh and can provide some pretty funny one liners. Of late, his accompaniment of a weird walk has been the phrase "'ave it large mother fu*kers".

He's a long-haired tree-hugging hippy-freak. Take a look at his web site.

Rachel

She's been my friend for years now, and we were good friends that so much so I took her to the prom at the end of West Mon. We have a photo of the day here.

Rachel goes to the college and not the 6th form that we go to, so I don't get to see her as much, but she occasionally invites me over to her house for a party with her other friends.

And as far as I know I'm the only one who hasn't fancied her out of the Cool Crew (all the others asked her out!!)

Robyn

Robyn. Blonde. The two go hand in hand. I don't mean that nastily, just Robyn has made us all laugh until we cry with some of the things she's said.

She was the only girl who came on holiday to Bude with us in the summer of 2002. (She had to come otherwise we wouldn't have had a tent :D)

I currently go to work with her on Saturdays in Llantarnam Grange Arts Centre in Cwmbran and it's lots of fun! Unfortunately for me, but fortunately for her she's got a Boyfriend and I don't get to see her as much now as I used to.

Hmm... seems to be happening to lots of my friends of late - imagine if I got a girlfriend - I wouldn't see any of them!
