Pay peanuts... get monkeys

WORK WORK WORK WORK play WORK WORK WORK WORK WORK....

Remind you of much? It does me. All I seem to do is work, and yet the money I earn gets spent before the next wage packet! Grrr...

On Saturdays I work in Llantarnam Grange Arts Centre Café/Restaurant. We are open from 10 AM to 4 PM and serve lunches all day. If you live near Cwmbran, then we are the big house opposite the bus shelter.

Plugging over. I work with Robyn (she got me the job there in all fairness) Bob and Phil. I love working there because its a chance for me to get to spend some time with people other than my friends or family - it brings a bit of variation into my everyday life! And I don't get more interaction with people than waiting on tables!

If Phil had a web site I'd put a link, but he aint so I wont.

During the summer hols instead of sitting on my arze all day, I work with my Dad. Well it's not technically with him, because I work in the labs in the same factory. For me its a great chance to learn more about science and how to use scientific equipment.

I definately noticed a change in the way I use the apparatus in school from using them in the labs. We test the quality of the product before it is allowed to be filled into the bottles.
