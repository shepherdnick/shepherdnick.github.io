Red vs Blue Tribute.

If you haven't already heard about the fastest growing thing on the internet of recent times, then you're gonna hear about it here first!

Red vs Blue is an online series of 5 minute shorts that take place in a fictional canyon called "Blood Gulch". Two teams - the reds and the blues each own a base and attempt to capture each others base, amongst telling a hillarious story. The reason it's so original is because the creators animations are restricted to the boundaries of a computer game - Halo.

The new type of animation is called "machinima" and is a similar style to puppeteering, seen as the animator has his limits like the boundaries of the game. I think it's fantastic. It must be to have made BBC News.
