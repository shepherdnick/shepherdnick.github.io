Me Muzic

Without music - life would be boring. Music is the soundtrack to your life. If you regret listening to music you liked three years ago, don't. You wouldn't have been you if you hadn't have listened to it back then.

I like most types of music. I don't only prefer a certain "Indie Classical Punk Melodic Power Metal Rock" genre. That's just silly. You just end up putting barriers up around yourself, and turning your ears off. I like to listen to things which my ears like. If its got a sexy bass line, and it sounds good - then listen to it!!! If its by a band that I've detested most of my life, but I like one song they've released, then I just listen to that song! It's not hard!
