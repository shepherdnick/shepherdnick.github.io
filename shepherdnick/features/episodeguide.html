Red vs Blue Episode Guide

Warning! This guide contains spoilers to the plot - read at your own risk!

Episode 1 - Why are we here?

The Reds start talking about why they have been posted in the Blood Gulch Canyon whilst the Blues look down on them through a sniper rifle.

Instead of fighting, which you would think they would be doing, they just stand around and talk. We also meet the Sarge.

Humerous Quote: "Whoop-de-f*!king-doo!"

Episode 2 - Red Gets a Delivery

Sarge is the greatest!! He tells the red team that they are to have upgrades to their team. A rookie.

But that's not all the upgrading they get, they also recieve the M12 LRV or as they quite rightly call it (after much deliberation) that its called a Puma.

Enter Lopez.

Humerous Quote: "Hey Simmons, what's the name of that mexican lizzard. Eats all the goats? Uh, that would be the chippacabre Sir, Hey Griff, How about that? Chippathingy. I like it. Got a ring to it."

Episode 3 - The Rookies

Enter Rookies. Yeah, both Blue and Red teams have rookies. The Red rookies called Donut, and Blue is called Caboose.

We find out that Caboose is annoying. But funny. And irritating.

The Reds send Donut out to the store to get headlight fluid and elbow grease. Donut is too busy thinking that Elbow Grease is fictional to realise there is no store.

Humerous Quote: "How long until you think he figures out there's no store? I'd say about a week"

Episode 4 - Head N00b in charge

Church gets p!ssed at Caboose and sends him in to look after the flag inside the base. Mistaking it for a store, Donut goes into the Blue base and takes the flag. Caboose mistakes him for the colonel and lets him take it.

Church desides to take the tank out for a spin when he realises, that no one in the base can drive it!

Humerous Quote: "Man, he's dumber than you are! No, you mean he's dumber than YOU are. WOW Tucker, great comeback"

Episode 5 - The Package is in the Open

The Blues find out that Donut took the flag and look on at him through the sniper scope.

The Blues use the teleporter to get to where Donut is faster. Tucker is scared to go through because the rocks they threw through the teleporter earlier came out hot and covered in black stuff.

Tucker doesnt make it to the other side of the transporter.

Humerous Quote: "It was four shots, like Blam Blam Blam. That's only three shots. Blam.

Episode 6 - 1.21 Gigawhats

Tucker comes out the other side of the teleporter - covered in black stuff. He thinks he has gone forward in time.

Whilst all this is going on, music gradually gets louder. It the radio on the Wathog. Simmons and Griff are in it and start blasting at the Blues.

Caboose decides to help them out by taking the tank.

Humerous Quote: "Stay here. Tank. Stay Here. Tank. Ahh, screw it!"

Episode 7 - Check out the threads on that Chick

The Blues fire on the Reds (taking cover behind a rock) and Caboose runs the tank driving tutorial.

The Blues give up firing. They turn around to find the tank cannon pointing in their faces.

We are told that in the next episode someone will die.

Humerous Quote: "We should have brought the tank. Yeah but no-one here knows how to drive it. I can see why hiding behind a rock is a much better strategy. OK I guess I gotta give that one to you."

Episode 8 - Don't PH34R the reaper

Major turning point in the plot. Well not really. Just really funny.

OK heres the situation. The Reds have a tanks cannon in their faces, whilst Tucker and Church are behind the rock, and Caboose is controlling the tank.

In confusion, the cannon switches to auto-fire mode and starts to fire on Grif as he tries to save his ass.

The cannon accidentaly aquires Church as its new target. The inevitable happens. He dies.

Humerous Quote: "You shot Church you team killing fUcktard!"
