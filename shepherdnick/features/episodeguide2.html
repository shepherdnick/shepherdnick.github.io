Red vs Blue Episode Guide

Warning! This guide contains spoilers to the plot - read at your own risk!

Episode 9 - After Church

The Reds make it back to their base, whilst the tank continues to fire on the Warthog (a.k.a. PUMA).

The Sarge sends in support and blows up Sheila (the tank).

Caboose escapes in the nick (*he he no pun intended*) of time.

Humerous Quote: "I'm scared Dave, Will I dream?, DAAAAAVEY, DAAAAAVEY"

Episode 10 - Shadow of his former self

The Blues call in for support, and they get sent Tex.

Before he arrives, they get a message from the other world. NO, not THAT world. The dead world. Church tells them about their new recruit - Tex.

He's a mutunous freak - by killing all of Church's former comrades. Church warns them to steer clear of Tex.

Humerous Quote: "I swear, your ass is haunted!"

Episode 11 - Knock Knock. Who's there. Pain.

Church was right. Tex is nuts. He uses Caboose as target practise.

Tex goes off to get the flag back. He doesn't talk much. But more than Lopez.

Tex throws a light grenade onto the side of Donut's face. They think its a spider. Then a blue pulsating thing. BOOM! End Ep.

Humerous Quote: "Don't kill me I'm too good looking to die!"

Episode 12 - Down but not out

The Blues watch as Tex kicks their ass. The Blue team's flag is returned.

Church returns and works out that Tex has gotten involved. Very involved. He's gone and gotten the flag back.

Sarge attempts to capture and hold hostage Tex. And knocks him out.

Only him isnt a him, him's a her.

Humerous Quote: "Yep, he's dead. Or Captured. Or Captured and Dead."

Episode 13 - Human Peer Bonding

Church tells the Blues that Tex was his last Girlfriend, and that she's mean. Real mean. She had cybernetic enhancements in order to make her better at combat.

Caboose really shows his intelligence in this ep. It's good, trust me.

Grif tries to be tough, but Tex is tougher. Church comes up with an idea to cause a distraction whilst he sneaks in and rescues Tex.

In order for them to get black armour, they have to go through the teleporter. "Oh Fuckberries"

Humerous Quote: "So how are you doing Caboose?, you following any of this? I think so... that guy Tex is really a robot... and you're his boyfriend... so that makes you... a Gay Robot. Yeah, that's right. I'm a Gay Robot."

Episode 14 - Roomier than it looks

Tucker goes through first and tells Caboose it doesnt hurt. It does. "You lied to me".

The Red's notice that there are more "special ops" coming towards them, and get ready. Caboose and Tucker fight about who talks to Church. Church meanwhile posseses Sarge's body.

Sarge sneaks into the base and helps Tex outside. Unaware of what's gone on inside, Caboose thinks he will help Church out and decides to snipe the Sarge.

Double jepardy?

Humerous Quote: "I'm just so mad about these God damn blues, I could spit. *spits*. Sir did you just spit in your own helmet? Uh... yeah. Permission to speak freely sir, but that's really fucking gross."
