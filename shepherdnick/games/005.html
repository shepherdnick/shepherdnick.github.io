F-0 GX.... I get the F-0, but what does the GX stand for?

Well, it stands for the version that is on the Gamecube. You see - that's one of the unique features of F-ZERO - you can save customised vehicles to your memory card, take the memory card to the arcade, plug your memory card into the arcade machine and upload your own racer/ character. How neat is that?

Well, I spose you're wonding what the verdict is on the new Ninty racer? It certainly kicks ass. I bet you're now thinking - but I would say that. I'm afraid it's not as good as I had hoped.

Graphics wise, absolutely stunning. It really shows how well the GC can handle lots of calculations. If you can imagine the sheer velocity of the game demands a very fast loading time of textures, polygons and the AI of the other racers. It handles these excruciatingly well. It really impressed me.

Sound: Like all futuristic racers, you can't get away without some slightly techno themes in there somewhere. But F-Zero not only has the techno themes, but it's mixed with some absolutely lush guitar riffs, to make a soundtrack which fits the game very well.

Where are the down sides for me then? Well, this game is no pick up and play. You have to sit down and work out how you want to handle the vehicles. For me, this is no problem, because I like to spend a bit of time figuring out how to control the game. But from the outset, the story mode is extremely difficult. It makes you feel like you're really bad at the game, and you forget about the story mode. You spend most of your time then playing the Grand-Prixes. But this is no easy task either. "Novice", is not really "Novice", more like "If-you-think-you-are-gonna-win-first-time-think-again".

I am now starting to get into how to play the game, and it is really fun to play. I sat back yesterday and watched someone else play it, and it seems faster from an onlookers POV. Just goes to prove how adrenaline pumping the game is then doesn't it? If you can't feel the speed you must be high on adrenaline!

I can imagine it taking donkeys years to complete properly, but once mastered, it will be amazing to show off in the arcades. If you want more info, click to go to the official site.
