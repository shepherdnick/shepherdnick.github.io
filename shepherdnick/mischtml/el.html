A page for the artist formerly known as El

Boyfriend to Ash, and all round cool girl, Eleanor Bradley, above anything else, shares a name with greats such as:


Eleanor Roosevelt,


Eleanor McEvoy,


Bradley Walsh

and of course, your friend and mine -


Thomas Bradley.

El got quite upset that her only mention was three words - and that was underneath Ash (wey hey!)

So here I am making a page all for her on my site. Thanks El. Make MORE work for me. Like as if I don't have enough to do! Well actually, I am making this page so I can't have THAT much to do.

hmm... what to say, what to say... oh I know!!

I like El! She's great to talk to - and I like talking to her because of the way she talks. I know it sounds weird but unlike most of today's youth she actually pronounces words properly - how they should be pronounced - and I love it. Although I suppose she is English:

Don't ask me why - just thought I'd chuck in a picture of an English Flag. I like pictures.

El's originally from "Up North" but I don't know exactly where... and she moved to a place that couldn't have better comedy value - that's right - Abersychan!!! Abersychan has many facilities such as a shop, fish and chip restaurant, a school and a leisure centre and many many other completely useful objects (and home to the great Teifi Lloyd - Ash knows him - he plays the bass).

Her family own a camper van. How cool is that? "uhh... it kinda had a vee dubyah on the front". You know back in the sixties they were called "Shaggin' Wagons".

A Rather Large Photo of a Camper Van which is not exactly the same colour as El's

You wanna know how even her job is cool? She works in a button shop. I'll say it again for those of you who weren't listening. She works in a BUTTON shop. If I could sell buttons, why I think I'd be the happiest man in the lower half of Pontypool! And she's an AVON lady.

Final Words: El's a charming, witty, intelectual, and socially acceptable people I know. Right then. There we have it. El's own page on "Shepherdnick". If you want me to write a page for you - then tough luck - the closing date was 2 minutes before you started reading this sentence.
