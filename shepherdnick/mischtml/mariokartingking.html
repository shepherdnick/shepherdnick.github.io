This page here's for the Mario Karting King

Last night we saw the reason gaming became popular. The much regarded Mario-Kart-don, who's hidden identity remains until tonight, as we can expose the man behind the mask, and ask him just how he feels about winning the "Mirror mode World Tour" competition that took place last night.

First up is the match (or should I say race) report direct from the road side with our special reporter for the competition, Wandering Goom, a member of the Goomba family. Hello to you.

Hello there, yes, I have to say, this years competition was an incredible experience for all involved! The trials and tribulations, the ups and the downs, it was an emotional rollercoaster!

The tournament started off quickly as usual, with Petey Piranah along with Toad getting a dash at the start. Once they were off, the battle progressed between these two prime racers throughout the early stages of the competition begging for someone to take a definite lead.

Then whilst on the DK Mountain course, a cleverly aimed "Greeno" as they are called, was fired from Toad straight into the back of Petey Piranah, sending him flying down the ranks, unable to recover. This cleverly put Toad at the top of the leader board, with 10 points in the clear. The competition seemed already won. Then things changed...

... A lack of quality driving by Toad made sure that he came a few positions under poll in the next two races meant that the points soon racked up again for Petey Piranah who conveniently won these races and ended up with four more points than Toad. This is how these competitions go, one minute your up there racing like a God, and the next you're ashamed of yourself.

It was not over yet however, Toad still had two more races to go. Dino Dino Land proved easy for both Toad and Petey, and on the last straight, with Petey in poll and Toad close behind, a nicely fired "Greeno" from Donkey Kong who was riding third, hit Petey from the course to his doom. Toad's win was in the bag. Now only two points separated the two Racing Kings.

It was, as classicly remembered, down to Rainbow Road. The music striked up, the lights counted down, the atmosphere was tense. "START" and they were off. Toad made a few fatal mistakes early on, and even fell off the track. It seemed everything was lost. But Oh No, In true sportsmanship style he carried on playing. The frantic shell throwing and banana dropping continued through the laps, and Toad kept on driving supremely, working his way through the ranks, and now in fourth. Just as the middle of the last lap came up, a sight which would have pleased anyone in his position was visible. A 1st person shell flew over the top, aimed at Petey. BOOM! The blue light from the explosion could be seen in the distance, and Toad soon overtook Petey, who seemed to loose it from then on. Toad swinged around the last corner, and finished in second place, with Petey no-where to be seen.

The tension remained as the race results were being tabulated. The final screen came on, and Toad was pleased to hear that he had won the Competition by one point. I can tell you, the race was incredibly exhilirating!

Thank you for that, Mr. Goom, a very accurate report of what happened out there. Now on to the most pressing question, who exactly was controlling Toads impecable driving during this rollercoaster race? Well my friends it gives me great pleasure in showing you, that in fact, BEN is this years MARIO KART KING!!
