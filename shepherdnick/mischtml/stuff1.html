Nice weather we're having

Noticed the hot weather recently? Bit of an odd thing to see here in Wales. And the really big thing that p!sses me off is the amount of people who complain about it!

Most of the year it rains. When the warm weather comes they complain that it's too hot! Make up your mind!! I love the hot weather and the sun and would enjoy it all year round. I know that some people would rather it being cold because you can always warm up - you can't always cool down. And that's fair enough. But please don't complain about both!

'nuff moaning now. What causes the hot weather, and what has caused the heatwave to occur in Europe this summer? It's a combination of things really. The mid-atlantic drifts from mid-america travel across the atlantic and hit Europe hard (called the prevailing wind). Whilst travelling across the ocean it usually picks up moisture and causes the normal British climate - warm yet wet. This is not so this year.

Most of the climate has been rolling in from the south and east where there are no oceans. The warm weather that occurs in the day can travel many miles across land, which it is unable to do over the oceans. This is not helped by large areas of high pressure which can keep the heat in and it stays in the same place.

This summer they appeared to have recorded the hottest temperature in Britain since the records began, but I fail to see that it could reach the previous record. The media had hyped up the temperture for days before and a story would not have been there if it weren't for the record, and that day in particular didn't seem any hotter than the days previous.

Anywho - I don't care about the height of the temperature, as long as we've got some temperature there to talk about!

Look out beach - here I come!
