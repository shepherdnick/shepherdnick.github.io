Valentines Day came and went like nobody's business. Not a scrap of it for me though. Does anybody actually know why the day exists, or is it just an excuse for card shops to make us spend more money. I spent the whole of the day in work anywho.

Over the weekend I bought Reservoir Dogs and Fawlty Towers (the two seasons) on DVD. It was a fun half term - I can say that! On the Monday of Half Term, I went to Cardiff with the lads (Plum, Dave and Ben) and we met these girls.... that was fun too. We went to have lunch in Wetherspoons after. Can't go wrong with a burger and a beer meal for only £3! We went to Tom's in the evening and I saw his amazing wireless network which he got hooked up to his laptop. It basically means he can access his computer from anywhere in the house! It's cool! I want one for my house! (I might be able to get broadband then!)

On wednesday evening, it was another lads night. I went out with Beechy and his Dad (my Dad went too) to see Wales vs. Scotland. What a great evening! Wales won 4-0 by the way, but the atmosphere in the stadium was amazing. I've been there when its full (70,000) but there were only 40,000 there on wednesday, and boy did they make some noise!

Now I move on to a night I won't forget in a long time. Thursday evening. Well, maybe I should start with the day time. I completed both XIII for the PC and I completed Mario Kart Double Dash completely. The evening was Leanne's 18th BDay party. The evening started off great. Drinking at the house I had a couple of shots and a few cans, until the coach arrived and we travelled to Newport Rugby Club. It was great in there too. £1.50 a pint. Icky Carling though! I didn't care, I was on it most of the evening. The dancefloor was pretty active for me for the rest of the evening aswell ;) .

I can't remember getting the coach home, although I do remember wandering around the house for a bit before falling asleep on the couch. I don't remember anything that happened between falling asleep and waking up looking out of the curtains the next day. I was awoken by horrible stories of what I had done the night before. I am so ashamed, embarrassed and dissapointed in what I did, that I don't want to tell you, you'll have to find out from someone else. All I want to say is sorry to everyone, especially Leanne, Ben, Rich and Sam who I know got the bad end of what I did.

I won't be getting that drunk for a time to come. Since then I've been trying to keep a low profile, but thanks to Ben who thinks it's hillarious, most of the McDonalds staff know, and probably most of school. I will not be enjoying tomorrow one bit.
