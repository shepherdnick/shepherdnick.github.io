OK - gonna try and make this as less amount of cheese as possible... :S

I'm a fourty-something west african fruit bat by the name of Billy. I grew up on a small farm just outside Chester. I'm currently in a job that suits my taste (I eat fruit, it has perks you know ;) ), have a beautiful wife, two twin daughters, pet gorillas (by the names Periwinkle, Charley and Mustard), four aunts, three uncles, no cousins, fifty-seven nephews, one niece, three grandparents, (I'm a new species apparently) and own a 485 brake horsepower, 4 litre engine, 40 cylinder, Turbo Diesel Injection Bat-mobile.

Oh... what's THAT? You want me to stop being sarcastic and cheesy with those really really bad personal profiles that seem to float about on the internet about EVERYONE and just tell you about me?

Ohkay then... whell, where shall I start?

I live in the land of the free, home of the brave - wait a min, that's Scotland isn't it? Well I don't live there. I live south-west of Scotland, on a little peninsula home to over three million people, mountains, sheep and where we like to think we're good at Rugby - yes, that's right - WALES!!!!

I know, I know, probably too passionate and patriotic yeah? Although on the other hand, that's not a bad description of what's actually in Wales. Not alot.

Well there's me of course. And my mates. And St. Albans RC High School, which is where we all go to the sixth form and learn lots of neat stuff. Ahem. What I really mean is, we TRY to "learn" "some" lots of our course in order to scrape a mark at the end!!

If you want a BIO (abbreviated from Biography) about me, then tuff luck mon amis - you aint getting it. And ya wanna know why? 'Cos I don't really know my height/hair colour/weight/size of feet/inside trouser leg/bowler hat preferences/etc. in order to give to you'. All you need to know about me is that I like to feed on sweet sweet peaches of the elderflower during the nights when the moon is fat, and people look upon it crying "Bloody good moon!" (Translates to English as - I'm a 17 year old bum who's got nothing better to do with his time than write a crappy web site about 'imself that probably no-one will ever see).

I would want to say more about like, where I've been living the past 17 years of my life, and what I've achieved from it, but quite frankly, I'm writing this at 11:00 at night, just done a full day's shift in my summer job, and I really can't be arsed!
