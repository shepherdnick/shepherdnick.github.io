Me and El

I had a phone call this afternoon from a friend, (but was scared to answer it at first thinking it was McDonalds asking me for a third night in a row to work...) and was happy to find out it was El. Random Phone calls are good. However, this was not so random as I had first suspected.

Someone had started spreading his or her muck (rumours) around the sixth form about me liking a certain person in a certain way. Or to put it another way, Ben Davies has been saying that I fancy El.

First I would just like to point out that I'm sorry to disappoint El, ('cos I know how much of a stud I am :P) but I don't think of El that way, and wouldn't choose to. I know how much she loves Ash and how much Ash loves her, and how bad things would be for anything to jeopardise that.

Secondly, I know I might seem to flirt with El, and other girls who I maybe shouldn't. But flirting is a form of communication. Friendly chatting etc. and there's no harm in it what-so-ever. I know my Dad flirts like the best of 'em in work - I've seen him. But it doesn't phase me, because I know he loves my Mum and incredible amount!

That said, I am quite disappointed in you Ben. I thought we are all mature enough to do and say things to one another without giggling like little schoolgirls. Obviously we are maturing at different rates, and some of us still can't accept that we can have sensible adult friendships without the need of lust or love.

Thirdly, I find it hard enough to talk to girls, especially ones I like, and making girls I already know paranoid about talking to me because they think I'm obsessed with them, does not make me feel any better. I can do without the malicious intent of rumours and rumour starters. A cheap joke for you can mean the end of a friendship for others.

I'm glad that El and I have cleared up the mess, and that both she and I can go on living without fearing that if we so much as talk to one another people would suspect we lust for each other. However, we needn't have had the conversation this afternoon if such allegations weren't made.

First, I'm sorry for the arguments. I know its bad to wash my dirty linen in public or whichever other cliché you'd like me to use, but I felt that I needed to let everybody know that I don't fancy El. One of the best ways to do this was on the forum - the only people on here are people that I know/ are close to me.

Second, I classed the 'observations' as rumours, because I had heard that I fancy El from more than one person previous to the phone call - how can people know about me before I've even heard of it myself? I'm not about to start naming names, so don't ask, but I DID hear some things about El and me before the phone call.

Third, I know you have tried to justify yourself Ben for just merely letting your observations and beliefs known, but really, did you think it through beforehand? Ash and El are together. They've been together for two years come September. By saying something like that did you think that it might have repercussions on their relationship? Lets say for arguments sake that I did fancy El. Where does that leave the relationship? It would mean El would hardly want to talk to me because of the awkward situation, and Ash would either be jealous or disown me as a friend. Just because you think something is right doesn’t give you the right to stick your nose in where it doesn’t belong.

Fourth, I believe that the relationship (friendship) that El and I had before this fiasco started will never be the same again. That’s not to say that I am not going to be friends with El, or with anyone else. It’s just that you have shown some people the light. People will always be watching the way I act around El from now on. You can say that no one cares and that I shouldn’t care what other people think, but people obviously do – look at the reaction on the forum.

I have learned a lesson from this episode – not to publicly distribute my feelings across the Internet. When I posted I was angry, and wasn’t thinking. I didn’t mean for the arguments or slanging matches to happen. In future I will personally sort out any such disputes.

Ben. Drew. Whoever it was. If you have something you believe is true, yet have no part to play in what happens, don’t say anything. You just hurt people otherwise.

I think this topic should die its death swiftly now however. It seems everyone has had his or her say. If it doesn’t people will just be repeating themselves. Everyone: hand shakes all round. Let’s leave it now.
